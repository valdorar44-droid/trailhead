## Description
Trailhead is an overland trip planner built for people who travel beyond the pavement.

Plan multi-day routes, find camps, check weather, save offline maps, build trail routes with pins, and listen to AI audio guides for nearby landmarks, parks, monuments, and historic places.

Features:
- AI trip planning with daily routes, camps, fuel, weather, and packing help
- Offline map, routing, trail, contour, and place downloads
- Trail route builder with pinned route previews
- Camp cards with photos, access notes, field reports, and admin/community edits
- Audio Guide Beta with Explore cards, summaries, and full stories
- What’s Around Me narration for your current location
- Community reports for road hazards, closures, water, trail conditions, and camp status
- Rig profile tools for clearance, range, drivetrain, tires, and recovery setup
- Saved routes, GPX import, favorites, and trip checklists

Trailhead is designed for overlanders, campers, road trippers, van travelers, off-road explorers, and anyone who wants better context before heading into remote areas.

Offline downloads are included. Some AI features and audio guide generation use credits or the Trailhead Explorer plan.

Always verify local rules, closures, fire restrictions, weather, road conditions, and land access before you travel. Trailhead is a planning aid, not a substitute for safe judgment in the field.
